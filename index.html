Blockchain at ASU is a student-led organization dedicated to exploring the future of decentralized technologies. Our mission is to educate, innovate, and connect students interested in Web3, smart contracts, and blockchain development.

Through our hands-on workshops and collaborative projects, we help students transition from curiosity to proficiency. Our community-driven approach ensures that everyone, from novices to experts, has the resources they need to succeed in the rapidly evolving blockchain landscape.

Whether you're a seasoned developer or completely new to crypto, we provide a welcoming community to learn, build, and thrive.

Our Achievements & Partnerships

We've collaborated with industry leaders to host impactful workshops and hackathons. Our members regularly participate in and win blockchain competitions statewide, building real-world decentralized applications (dApps) on networks like Ethereum, Solana, and SUI.
